About



K-pop duo EXO-SC is the second official sub-unit of the South Korean boy band . Following their first official offshoot, , EXO-SC made their debut in 2019. Comprising founding members Sehun and Chanyeol, the pair -- also known as "Exo-Se-Chan" -- issued its first EP, What a Life, in summer 2019 on parent label . Topping the Korean Gaon chart and landing in the Top 10 of the U.S. Heatseekers and World Albums charts, the six-song set remained close to 's typical sound, incorporating elements of R&B, hip-hop, and electronic pop. The EP was produced with Korean hip-hop artist and included the singles "Just Us 2" and "Closer to You."

The following year, the pair returned with their first official album 1 Billion Views. Featuring guest appearances by Penomeco, , and returning producer , the LP also included solo tracks from Sehun ("On Me") and Chanyeol ("Nothin'"). ~ Neil Z. Yeung, Rovi

MILESTONES

Receive RIAA platinum album certification in the US

In January 2020, it was announced that Love Yourself: Answer sold 1 million units in the US, making it officially platinum certified by the RIAA. 1 unit is 1 album sale, 10 songs sold from 1 album, or 1,500 audio or video streams. The album was also the first by a Korean act to receive BPI certification (silver) in the UK.

Perform at the Grammys

BTS performed at the 62nd Grammy Awards with Lil Nas X and a variety of other artists. While they didnâ€™t get to have their own stage, they still made history as the first Korean act to perform at the prestigious awards show. They were also the first Korean act to present at the ceremony back in 2019.

Reach #1 on the Billboard 200 chart

In 2018, Love Yourself: Tear shot to a #1 debut on the Billboard 200 album chart, making them the first Korean group to achieve the feat. They went on to top it again with Love Yourself: Answer and Map of the Soul: Persona. BTS was also the first Korean act to reach #1 on the Billboard Artist 100 and chart multiple albums on the Billboard 200 in one year.

Surpass 5 billion Spotify streams

After the release of Map of the Soul: Persona, BTS surpassed a staggering 5 billion streams on Spotify. Just a few months earlier, they also became the first Korean act to reach 1 billion streams on Apple Music.

Perform a stadium show in the US

When BTS added a show at NYCâ€™s Citi Field Stadium to their Love Yourself tour, they made history as the first Korean act to ever perform a US stadium show. Not only did the show sell out, BTS later went on to become the first group to sell out stadium shows in London and Paris on not one but two nights each.
