MILESTONES

EXO’s Baekhyun Is 1st Solo Artist Ever To Receive Triple Platinum Certification From Gaon

On July 9, Gaon Chart announced that EXO’s Baekhyun’s latest mini album “Delight” had received an official triple platinum certification after selling over 750,000 copies since its release in May—making Baekhyun the first solo artist ever to receive the distinction.

EXO's Baekhyun is officially a 'Million-selling' solo artist with his 2nd mini album 'Delight'!

As of June 30, EXO's Baekhyun has officially become a 'Million-selling' solo artist! According to SM Entertainment, Baekhyun's recently released 2nd solo mini album 'Delight' has recorded a total of 1,018,746 copies sold as of June 30. This is the first time in approximately 19 years that a solo artist has surpassed 1 million album sales, after Kim Gun Mo in 2001.

EXO’s Baekhyun Sets New Record By Becoming Million Seller Soloist

EXO’s Baekhyun reaches greater heights as his latest solo album achieves more than one million sales. As of June 30, Baekhyun’s second mini-album, Delight, recorded a sales volume of 1,018,746 physical copies.
