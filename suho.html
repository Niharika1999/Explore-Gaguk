About



Kim Jun-myeon, better known by his stage name Suho, is a South Korean singer, songwriter and actor. He is the leader and main vocalist of the South Korean-Chinese boy group Exo and its sub-unit Exo-K. Apart from his group's activities, Suho has also starred in various television dramas and movies such as One Way Trip, The Universe's Star, Rich Man, Middle School Girl A, and How Are U Bread. He debuted as a soloist on March 30, 2020 with the release of his EP, Self-Portrait.
